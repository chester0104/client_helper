## Common Client Questions

### How do I place an order?
Visit meridian-supply.example.com/order, add items to your cart, and check out.
Business clients can request net-30 invoicing at checkout.

### What is your return policy?
Unused widgets can be returned within 30 days for a full refund. Start a return
at meridian-supply.example.com/returns using your order number.

### How do I track my order?
Use the tracking link in your confirmation email, or enter your order number at
meridian-supply.example.com/track.

### How do I reset my account password?
Go to meridian-supply.example.com/login, click "Forgot password", and follow the
emailed instructions.
